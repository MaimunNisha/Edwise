What We Offer

Test Prepartion

We provide the best training for IELTS, TOEFL, PTE, GMAT, GRE, SAT and ACT. Every student is unique and we follow a mentoring model where every student is given personal attention.Our batch size is small and our faculty ensures that the students performs to the best of their ability.

Career Counselling

We guide you though a maze of questions to find solutions best suited to your profile ensuring you choose the best academic path to achieve your career goals based on your aptitude, ability and interest.

Finance & Scholarship

We give complete guidance on Educational Loans offered by Financial Institute and Banks. We also provide information to students about the various International, Institutional and National Scholarships.

Admission Guidance

We give special attention to your applicataion whether it is paper based or online. We see to it that your required documents are thoroghly compiled, attested, highlighting the areas essential for a well presented error free applicataion.

Popular Courses

Popular courses vary from country to country. We assist in identifying what are the industry trends and guide you in subject selection based on parameters of skills required in the industry. We also help to identify the correct fa-institutions for these popular courses.
